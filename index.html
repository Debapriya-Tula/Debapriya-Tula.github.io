I'm an Engineering graduate from IIIT-Sri City where I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering. I am currently working as a Machine Learning Engineer at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), Hyderabad. Before joining TCS, I was working as an AI software developer at LimeChat, where I was involved in designing contextual chatbots.

My areas of interest include Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. I am passionate about research in the aforementioned areas. I am currently exploring the fields of Model Compression and Curriculum Learning.

I am also working on a project which involves reducing the frame rate in videos followed by frame reconstruction for efficient (internet) data usage.
